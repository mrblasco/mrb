Andrea Blasco

I am a Fellow at Harvard University's Institute of Quantitative Social Science (IQSS) and a postdoctoral researcher at the NASA Tournament Lab (NTL) where I am designing experiments and performing quantitative analysis of behavior in crowdsourcing contests.

I am an economist and my research interests include topics in Industrial organization, Economics of Innovation, Media Economics, Matching markets, Crowdsourcing, Economic models of contests.
